Huckleberry Hunter
About

Huckleberry hunter is a data driven plant locator focusing on edible fruit and nut bearing perennials. The app predicts plant distributions and uses these predictive models to identify which of Massachusetts' public open spaces are likely to be home to these plants. The data powering this app come from a few places. It uses plant identifications from fallingfruit.org and inaturalist.org, in combination with climate data from the PRISM climate group and the 2016 National Land Cover Database. The underlying code to produce this app is here: github.com/ebuz/huckleberry_hunter.

Please forage and use Huckleberry hunter responsibly. This app focuses on plants with edible fruits, nuts and seeds since collecting from these plants should not harm the plant. With that in mind, please do not harm any plants you find using this app, neither the ones you collect from or any of their neighbors. Be sure to follow any local signs and guidelines when foraging; treat our outdoors with care.
